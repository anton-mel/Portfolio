Check the cruelty-free status of the brands
faster with TurBOTa for Chrome!

Acquiring products that are not tested on animals encourages brands to move to a cruelty-free philosophy and be certified by PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals). In-vitro testing and safe micro-dosing of human volunteers can replace cruel and immoral animal testing. The culture of cruelty-free consumption is not widespread in Ukraine today, so our team is inspired by the fact that TurBOTA will accelerate its development among the younger generation. Download this extension to be aware of brands that ignore this issue or use non-natural materials for their products.

TurBOTa extension does NOT collect users' data and is free of advertisements.
